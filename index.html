? CALCULATION METHODOLOGY
Test Positivity Rate (%) = (Σ Confirmed / Σ Tested) × 100

The percentage of tested patients who had a confirmed malaria result. Capped at 100%. Rate = 0 if tested = 0.

Step 1 – Filter facilities

Only keep HFs with at least 1 month where tested > 0 across the entire period. HFs with tested = 0 for all months are excluded.

Step 2 – Row-level values

For each row read confirmed and tested. Convert nulls to 0.

Step 3 – Facility-level monthly aggregation

Group by hf × year × month. Sum confirmed and tested. Calculate test_positivity_rate = MIN(100, (confirmed / tested) × 100) (0 if tested = 0).

Step 4 – Location-level monthly aggregation

Group by location × year-month. Sum confirmed and tested across facilities. Recalculate rate.

Step 5 – Location-level yearly aggregation

Group by location × year. Sum and recalculate. Used for yearly heatmap view.

Step 6 – Heatmap

Locations sorted high→low by average positivity rate. Higher = greater proportion of tested patients with confirmed malaria.
